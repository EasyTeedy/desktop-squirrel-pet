# desktop-squirrel-pet


🐿️ Desktop Squirrel Pet

A small animated squirrel that lives on your desktop and keeps you company.

The squirrel moves back and forth along the bottom of your screen, hopping around in a natural and playful way. It is primarily designed for macOS, but can be adapted to other systems by adjusting the x and y starting position values. You should also update the movement boundaries to define how far left and right the squirrel is allowed to travel.

Its behavior is dynamic and slightly unpredictable:
the squirrel can hop, stop, look around, dig, and even eat an acorn after digging.
These actions are controlled using Python’s random.choice() to create more natural movement patterns.

🖱️ Interaction

You can interact with the squirrel using your mouse:

Clicking on it will trigger a hurt animation
Be careful: clicking too often may cause the squirrel to "die" 💀

If that happens, simply restart the program and it will come back to life.
